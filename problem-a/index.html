MuLan

Mulan is a character in both Disney animated film called MuLan, and the 2020 version film with the same name MuLan, which is the live-action adaptation of that animated film.

Mulan's story is based on a real ancient Chinese warrior. Once upon a time, Chinese ancient empire need to fight a war, which required every family to contribute one men to join the army. Mulan's family only has one man, which is her dad who is too old to fight in a war. To protect her family, Mulan pretent to be a man and join the army to save her family. She then become a bravest and best warrior.

Why I admire Mulan?

  • Mulan is brave. She is willing to stand out for her family.
  • Mulan shows us the possibility of women become the best warrior. She demonstrates female power.
  • She is strong-minded. When she choose to become a warrior, she will be the best. She didn't skip training or being afraid using excuses that she is a women.
